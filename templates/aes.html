Real Encryption

  • Algorithm: AES-256-GCM (authenticated encryption)
  • Key derivation: Scrypt with parameters n=2^14, r=8, p=1
  • Random salt: 16 bytes per encryption
  • Random nonce: 12 bytes per encryption
  • Strength: Depends on password quality. Use 12+ characters for good security.

Important Notes

  • Save your bundle JSON safely if you want to decrypt later
  • Password cannot be recovered if forgotten
  • Each encryption generates new random salt and nonce
  • Don't share passwords through unsecured channels
